What is Lean Six Sigma?

Lean Six Sigma is a data-driven approach that combines two methodologies:

  • Lean focuses on eliminating waste and streamlining processes, ensuring that every step in the process adds value.
  • Six Sigma uses statistical tools to reduce variability and defects, ensuring high levels of consistency and quality.

Together, they create a robust framework for businesses to optimize operations, enhance quality, and deliver greater value to customers.
